SCM Hatching

The SCM Hatching routine takes an assignment of patterns and makes them available based upon the scale of the drawing being hatched. While in no way as powerful as AutoCad's hatching command, it retains the settings making it an excellent “first hatch” tool.
 

Video demonstration previously available here required Adobe Flash Player, which is no longer supported by modern browsers.

Here, ten patterns are inserted without entering parameters or opening a single dialog box. Predefined patterns and shading can save significant time and re-printing. More SCM videos here...

 

More Features

  1. Hatching customization is a very cost effective way to promote consistency.
  2. Hatching is linked to the drawing scale.

  3. Hatching automatically layers.
